About Me  

I am a junior developer at Xactware helping create the next generation fully-cycle claims analytical platform and reporting tool for the global property insurance industry. As a self-taught engineer I am a highly driven self starter with passion to continously learn and refine my skills. I write highly readable, maintainable and efficient code.

I grew up near Seattle, Washington. After living for two years in South America I made my way to Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah to pursue a degree in Exercise Science. Shortly before graduation I caught the coding bug while working a sales job for InsideSales.com. Instead of selling the product I wanted to help create it. Soon after leaving that job I got to work learning web development and quickly fell in love with it.
